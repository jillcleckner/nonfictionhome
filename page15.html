Research Paper II

Idea formation
Information, digging for
In the library – hours
Abstract, introduction, body, conclusion.

           Survive, how will I
           The deadlines, the due dates
           Numb is my brain, alive it barely feels
           Thesis statement, works cited page, APA, MLA.

                         Energy – every ounce depleted
                         My sleep invaded
                         My life – overwhelming
                         Rough draft, edit, final copy.
                                                                                    Turn it in. 

Jill Cleckner
